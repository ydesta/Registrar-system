Welcome to HiLCoE

HiLCoE (HiLCoE College) is a premier institution dedicated to excellence in technology and engineering education. Founded in 1999, our institution has been at the forefront of computer science education in Ethiopia for over two decades, committed to providing cutting-edge education and fostering innovation through modern teaching methods and state-of-the-art facilities.

With a distinguished history of academic excellence and a focus on practical, industry-relevant education, we prepare our students to become leaders in the rapidly evolving technology sector. Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure students learn the most current and relevant skills.

We believe in the power of technology to transform lives and communities. Through our comprehensive programs, state-of-the-art facilities, and dedicated faculty, we empower students to innovate, create, and lead in the digital world.
